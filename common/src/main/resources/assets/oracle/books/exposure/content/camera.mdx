## Attachments
There are 4 types of attachments you can put on/in the camera:
- [Film](attachments/film)
- [Flash](attachments/flash)
- [Lens](attachments/lens)
- [Filter](attachments/filter)

## Camera Controls
- Shutter Speed (brightens or darkens the image)
- Composition Guide
- Flash *on/off/auto* (if installed)

## Selfie Mode
For those who want to be the main subject of a photo - there is a selfie mode.
- Accessed by the F5 key (by default).
- When looking through Viewfinder - F5 will toggle only between first-person and third-person-front views.
