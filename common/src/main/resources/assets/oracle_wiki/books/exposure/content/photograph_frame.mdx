### Fancy display for your [Photographs](photograph).

- Has three sizes: 1x1, 2x2, 3x3.
- Placed similar to Paintings, in that it would fill all available space that it can.
    - But unlike a Painting, Photograph Frame can be placed on floor and ceiling as well.
- Use a Glow Ink Sac on a frame to make it glow.
- Has Glass variant, in which Photograph covers all space and frame is not visible.r
