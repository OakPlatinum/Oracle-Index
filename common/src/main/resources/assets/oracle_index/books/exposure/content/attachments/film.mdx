<Asset location="exposure:black_and_white_film" width={40}/> Black and White Film <Asset location="exposure:color_film" width={40}/> Color Film

These are inserted into a [Camera](../camera) and store your exposures. Single roll can store up to **16** frames.

You can quickly rename the film by ***using*** the item.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Renaming this way does not cost experience and can be of a great help to identify/organize rolls.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• Renamed rolls keep their new name when developed.

## Developing
<Asset location="exposure:developed_black_and_white_film" width={40}/>Developed Black and White Film <Asset location="exposure:developed_color_film" width={40}/>Developed Color Film

When you are finished with the roll, you need to develop it to make it suitable for printing in [Lightroom](../lightroom).

<Asset location="exposure:black_and_white_film" width={40}/> **Black and White Film** is developed by crafting it with just a single **Water Bottle**.\
<Asset location="exposure:color_film" width={40}/> **Color Film** is developed by crafting it with **Awkward Potion**, **Thick Potion** and **Mundane Potion**.
